About Me

I started learning web development about a year ago. After working on some informal projects on my own, I read a newspaper article about Holberton Tulsa. It seemed like a great fit and an amazing opportunity for me. I've enjoyed me time at Holberton and have certainly learned a lot. I have completed coursework using C, Python, bash scripts, MySQL, React JS, TypeScript, amomng others. I decided to focus on Front-End web development because I have previously worked in customer service roles and Front-End made the most logical sense with my previous employment experiences.
